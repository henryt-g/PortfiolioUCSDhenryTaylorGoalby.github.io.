ABOUT

I am a student at the University of California, San Diego working on policy analysis, development, and advocacy at the City and State levels. Before my most recent jobs at the City of San Diego and as Housing Affairs Director for UCSD, I was a student representative for the Tigard-Tualatin School District focused on researching for, writing, and passing pro-student policies. My work has been featured on news at the local and state levels, although most of it is goes uncovered due to the nature of my work itself.

Heavily involved in student advocacy, I believe adamantly in the transformative power of learning. I've worked at the local and state school board levels part-time and have given multiple talks on student-centered policy and high school educational development.
